

# Vyzora.ai — AEO/GEO Optimization Agency Website

## Brand & Design
- **Name:** Vyzora.ai
- **Style:** Dark background with warm accent colors (amber/orange tones), clean typography, premium feel
- **Vibe:** Authoritative, future-forward, urgent but genuine — like a top-tier consulting firm meets cutting-edge AI

---

## Page Structure (Single-page scrolling site)

### 1. Hero Section
- Bold headline communicating urgency: "AI is rewriting how customers find businesses. Is yours visible?"
- Subheadline explaining AEO/GEO in simple terms
- Email capture CTA prominently placed ("Get Your Free AI Visibility Audit")
- Subtle animated background or gradient to convey tech sophistication

### 2. The Problem
- Clear, concise section explaining why traditional SEO isn't enough anymore
- Stats/facts about AI-driven search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- Creates urgency — companies not optimizing now will fall behind

### 3. What We Do — Sprint-Based Services
- **AEO/GEO Sprint** — Fast, results-driven engagement (4-6 weeks) to get your brand visible in AI answers
- **Retainer / Consulting** — Ongoing optimization, AI strategy consulting
- **In-House Training** — Teach your team to leverage AI effectively
- Presented as clean cards with icons and brief descriptions

### 4. Expert Quotes & Social Proof
- Curated quotes from AI leaders and industry experts about the importance of AEO/GEO (attributed to real public figures like Sam Altman, Rand Fishkin, etc.)
- Designed as elegant quote cards with warm accent styling

### 5. Testimonials
- 3-4 realistic client testimonials highlighting sprint results
- Company type, role, and outcome — focused on measurable impact
- Clean card layout with subtle trust indicators

### 6. Why Now? (Urgency Section)
- Timeline/stats showing AI search adoption growth
- "Early movers are capturing visibility that will be hard to win later"
- Another email CTA embedded here

### 7. How It Works
- Simple 3-step process: **Audit → Sprint → Dominate**
- Clean visual flow showing the client journey

### 8. Final CTA Section
- Strong closing headline reinforcing urgency
- Email capture form (same as hero)
- "Limited sprint slots available" messaging

### 9. Footer
- Vyzora.ai branding
- Simple links (Privacy, Terms)
- Social links placeholders

---

## Key Design Details
- Dark background (#0a0a0a range) with warm amber/orange accents
- Modern sans-serif typography (Inter or similar)
- Smooth scroll animations as sections come into view
- Fully responsive — mobile-first approach
- Email captures use toast notifications for success feedback
